SACRED TRADITION

The Essence of Devotion

﴿ إِنَّ اللَّهَ مَعَ الصَّابِرِينَ ﴾

Eid ul-Adha, the "Festival of Sacrifice," honors Prophet Ibrahim's (AS) absolute submission to Allah. When commanded to sacrifice his son, his unwavering faith transformed into divine mercy — a ram replaced Ismail (AS).

🤍

Spiritual Lesson

True sacrifice isn't about loss, but surrendering what we hold dearest for Allah's pleasure.

"Indeed, Allah is with the patient." (Qur'an 2:153)

Story of Ibrahim (AS)

🌙✨

The Divine Vision

Prophet Ibrahim saw a recurring dream instructing him to sacrifice his son. In prophetic tradition, dreams of prophets are revelation.

🕊️💬

The Conversation with Ismail

"O my father, do as you are commanded. You will find me, insha'Allah, among the patient." A moment of absolute devotion.

🐏🤲

The Miracle of Sacrifice

As Ibrahim placed the knife, Allah replaced his son with a magnificent ram. Willingness and faith are more precious than blood.

Qurbani: The Act of Giving

One third for family, one third for friends, one third for the needy.

❤️‍🔥

Faith

🍖🍛

Charity

Unity

Global Muslim brotherhood, compassion, and shared remembrance.
